ABOUT

I am Agniva Si an undergraduate Metallurgy student of the Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee. I have been designing for more than 4 years and have been sketching from my childhood. Designing became a passionate hooby when I was recruited for the Software Development Section, IIT - Roorkee a.k.a. SDSLabs.

Starting from Illustrations to UI/UX designing I have worked on major projects of my instituttion and have been active in freelancing also. Apart from designing I also have a good experience with Digital Painting. Currently I own an Intuos Manga tablet.

I currently have a fair knowledge in Image editing, cartoon illustrations, UI designing, Icon art, Typography and many more.
